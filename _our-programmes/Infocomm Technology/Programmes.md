<h3><strong>Vision</strong></h3>
<p>Empowering our students through <strong>innovative teaching and learning</strong>,
dedicated to nurturing<strong> Future-ready, Responsible Digital Learners </strong>and <strong>Creative Collaborators.</strong>
</p>
<h3><strong>Mission</strong></h3>
<p>In line with MOE EdTech Masterplan 2030, the four pillars of our mission
are:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Cultivate ACSians to be digitally-empowered, future-ready learners and
innovators</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Empower teachers to be technologically-adept, collaborative learning designers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provide intelligent, responsive, digitally-equipped learning environment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Partner parents to build a networked EdTech ecosystem.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>ACS(P)'s ICT curriculum builds digital literacy, critical thinking, problem-solving
and creativity. Students progressively develop ICT skills from basics to
advanced concepts throughout their primary years.</p>
<p>Digital literacy is defined as a set of knowledge, skills and dispositions
that help our learners become confident, critical and responsible users
of digital technologies. Through our key programmes, we focus on developing
9 core digital competencies, aligned with the EdTech Masterplan 2030's
"Find, Think, Apply, Create" framework.</p>

<p><strong>Key ICT Programmes</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>ICT Baseline Programme (P1–6):</strong> Interactive lessons and
online self-directed learning covering essential technological skills including
touch typing, document processing, and presentation applications.</p>
<p><strong>Cyber Wellness:</strong> Interactive programmes that teach students
online etiquette, cyberbullying awareness, and personal information protection.</p>
<p><strong>Coding &amp; Computational Thinking (P4 &amp; 5):</strong> Students
learn block-based coding and develop computational thinking skills.</p>
<p>At ACS(P), we leverage ICT to inspire curiosity, collaboration, and innovation,
preparing students as effective and responsible digital learners for an
ever-evolving digital future.</p>
