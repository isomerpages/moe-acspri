### **FPSP Competition 2014**
Six of our boys participated in the 6th Future Problem Solving Program (FPSP) National Finals held at Raffles Girls’ School from 30th Oct to 1st Nov 2014. The topic for the competition was ‘Land Transportation’. The boys applied the 6-step problem solving method to address the future scenario and they did us proud by getting the following achievements:

Global Issues Problem Solving Team (Junior)  – 3rd Place

Global Issues Problem Solving Team (Junior) Action Plan Presentation – 2nd Place

Global Issues Problem Solving Alternate (Junior) – 1st Place (Navin Sivakumar)
